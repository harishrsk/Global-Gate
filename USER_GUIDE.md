# Global-Gate User Guide: From Harvest to Hub 🌾📦

Welcome to Global-Gate. This guide will help you understand how to navigate the platform and use its specialized features to scale your agricultural trade.

---

## 1. Getting Started 🏁
When you first log in, you'll see the **Agriculture Trade Hub**. This is your command center.
- **Overview**: Shows your active routes and any reports waiting for your approval.
- **Quick Stats**: Monitor your throughput and pending tasks at a glance.

## 2. Setting Up Trade Corridors 🗺️
A "Corridor" is a specific trade route (e.g., Exporting Coffee from Brazil to the USA).
1. Go to **Trade Corridors** in the sidebar.
2. Click **"New Corridor"**.
3. Enter the Product, Origin, and Destination.
4. Once created, click **"View Regulations"** to see exactly what certificates and safety standards you need to follow for that specific route.

## 3. The AI Vision Lab 📸
This is where the magic happens. Use this to verify product quality before shipping.
1. Go to **AI Vision Lab**.
2. Upload a clear photo of your product (e.g., a crate of peppers).
3. Click **"Start Vision Analysis"**.
4. **AI Output**:
   - **Grading**: Tells you if the product is Premium or Standard.
   - **Market Intel**: Tells you which countries have high demand for this product right now.
   - **Paperwork**: Gives you a checklist of every document you need to prepare for export.

## 4. Approving Reports & PQC Security 🛡️
As a manager, you must approve the AI's findings before a batch is cleared for export.
1. Go to the **Overview**.
2. Click on a pending report in the **Verification Queue**.
3. Review the details.
4. Click **"Approve & Sign"**.
   - **What is PQC?**: When you click approve, the system creates a "Post-Quantum Signature." This is a futuristic type of security that ensures your signature can never be forged or altered, even by super-powerful future computers.

## 5. Compliance Feed 🔔
Stay updated with global trade changes.
- If the EU changes their pesticide limits or the UAE updates their import tariffs, you will see a notification here. 
- The system automatically filters these alerts based on your **Active Corridors**.

## Frequently Asked Questions ❓

**Q: Why do I need a Phytosanitary Certificate?**
A: Most countries require this to prove your product is free from pests and diseases. The AI Vision Lab will tell you exactly which one you need.

**Q: Can I track more than one product?**
A: Yes! You can add as many Corridors as you like for different products and routes.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Absolutely. Global-Gate uses Enterprise-grade encryption and PQC signing to ensure your trade secrets and audit trails are immutable.
